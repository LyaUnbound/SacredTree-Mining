# SacredTree Mining

Mining ores from stone. What ores have a chance to drop depends on the stone you are mining.
Has a chance to randomly drop. How many you get depends on the ore and stone mining from. Works with fortune. Silk touch gives no ores.

## Overworld
Stone
- coal
- raw copper
- raw iron

Andesite
- lapis lazuli
- raw copper
- raw iron

Diorite
- redstone
- coal
- raw copper

Granite
- emerald
- raw copper
- raw iron

Terracotta
- coal
- raw gold
- gold nugget

## Nether
Basalt, Blackstone, Netherrack
- gold nugget
- quartz
- netherite scrap
